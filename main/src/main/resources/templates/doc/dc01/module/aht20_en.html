MODULE_AHT20 Module Introduction

Module Description

The product is pre-installed with the AHT20 module for measuring temperature and humidity. Since the product generates heat during operation, the measured temperature will be slightly higher than the ambient temperature. Click here to view the sensor performance specifications.
