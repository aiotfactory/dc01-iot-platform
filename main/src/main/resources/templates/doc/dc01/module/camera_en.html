MODULE_CAMERA Module Introduction

Module Description

The DC01 product has a camera interface. Although it can capture video, the performance is poor and it is generally used for taking photos. The lens can be replaced with ordinary, fisheye, infrared, etc., usually equipped with an ov2640 or ov5640 camera at the factory. Click here to view the MODULE_CAMERA section for configuration.

Data Upload - Automatic

The camera can take photos on schedule and report JPEG images. Click here to view the MODULE_CAMERA section for configuration. You can check the most recent captured images in the module page.

Data Upload - Command

By sending commands to the device will also trigger the device to upload data. Click here to view the CAMERA camera photo chapter to understand which commands can be executed and the format of the uploaded data.

Click here to view how to configure the data forwarding of the module. Click here to view the format of the forwarded data.

Runtime Information

Pins Camera Interface Used Pins, if not installing a camera, these pins can be used for other purposes.

Camera Status Example {"times_failed":0,"times_inited":1,"times_take":19}, indicating that since the camera was turned on, its status, times_failed failure count, times_inited whether already initialized ready, times_take number of photos taken.
